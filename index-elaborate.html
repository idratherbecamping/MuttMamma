The Method™

Your Dog's 21-Day Journey

Our proven transformation program breaks down complex behavioral issues into manageable phases, ensuring lasting results for both you and your dog.

1-7

Foundation Phase

Establishing trust, leadership, and basic obedience. Your dog learns to respect boundaries and begins responding to fundamental commands.

  • Leadership establishment
  • Basic commands (sit, stay, come)
  • Boundary setting
8-14

Breakthrough Phase

Addressing specific behavioral issues head-on. This is where we see the most dramatic improvements in problem behaviors.

  • Aggressive behavior modification
  • Leash training mastery
  • Socialization protocols
15-21

Mastery Phase

Perfecting behaviors and ensuring long-term success. Your dog becomes a well-balanced, confident companion ready for any situation.

  • Advanced command reliability
  • Owner education & handoff
  • Lifetime support setup
